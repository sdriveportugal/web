Reduced Visibility

773: Poor visibility conditions for driving are considered to exist:

A. Whenever there are a large number of vehicles in circulation
B. Whenever the driver cannot see the traffic lane for a length and width of at least 50 metres
C. Whenever the vehicle has not had its periodic inspection

Definition:

Article 19 — Reduced or insufficient visibility

For the purposes of this Code, visibility is considered reduced whenever the driver cannot see the entire width of the roadway for at least 50 m.

Why option B is incorrect:

It incorrectly refers to a single "traffic lane" rather than the entire roadway.

It also mentions a "width of at least 50m", which is unrealistic (a lane is only ~3.5m wide).

Why option A is correct:

When there is heavy traffic, visibility of the road width and 50m ahead is reduced, matching the legal definition.

Note: IMT often uses tricky wording. Always refer to the exact Highway Code definition.

Reduced Visibility — दृश्यता घट्ने अवस्था

773: Poor visibility conditions for driving are considered to exist:

A. Whenever there are a large number of vehicles in circulation
B. Whenever the driver cannot see the traffic lane for a length and width of at least 50 metres
C. Whenever the vehicle has not had its periodic inspection

IMT ले दिने यस्ता प्रश्नहरूमा शब्दहरूलाई कसरी घुमाएर राखिएको हुन्छ भन्ने कुरामा ध्यान दिनु जरुरी हुन्छ।

परिभाषा:

Article 19 — Reduced or insufficient visibility

दृश्यता घटेको (reduced) मानिन्छ जब चालकले सम्पूर्ण सडकको चौडाइ कम्तीमा ५० मिटर दूरीसम्म देख्न सक्दैन।

  • पूरा सडकको चौडाइ देख्न नसकिएको हुनु
  • कम्तीमा ५० मिटर अगाडि देख्न नसकिएको हुनु

उत्तर B किन गलत?

B ले "traffic lane" (एउटा मात्र लेन) को कुरा गर्छ, तर परिभाषाले "पूरा सडक" भन्न्छ।

त्यसमा "५० मिटर चौडा ट्राफिक लेन" को कुरा गरिएको छ, जुन असम्भव छ। एउटा लेनको चौडाइ ~३.५ मिटर मात्र हुन्छ।

उत्तर A किन सही?

धेरै ट्राफिक हुँदा वा खराब मौसम हुँदा दृश्यता घट्छ। यस अवस्थामा सडकको चौडाइ र ५० मिटर दूरी देख्न सकिँदैन, जसले परिभाषा पूरा गर्छ।

नोट: IMT ले प्रश्नलाई जटिल बनाउन गलत शब्दावली प्रयोग गर्न सक्छ। सधैं Highway Code को सही परिभाषामा भर पर्नुहोस्।
